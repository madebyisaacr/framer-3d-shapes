# 3D Shapes Framer Plugin

3D Shapes is a free Framer plugin with hundreds of abstract 3D visuals you can drag and drop into your projects. Browse a wide range of styles and instantly enhance your designs.

[Open on Framer Marketplace](https://framer.link/lLfQvxm)
